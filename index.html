TRIPS,TREKS & TOURS!

Every Year,the Technology Adventure Society,IITKharagpur,organises the trek Events for winter as well as the summer.Some memomrable treks since last two years being the kedartal trek,kuari pass trek and fachu kandi pass trek.Real essence of a trip lies in meeting new people, exchanging stories and creating memories/stories with them.Many Road Trips are also organised suc as the bhutan road trip organised last year.

On-Campus Events

Technology Adventure Society,IITKharagpur,organised various ground events in the IIT campus.The events are really interesting and mind relaxing for the participants at a very low participating cost which is subsidised under the Technology Students' Gymkhana ,IIT KHARAGPUR.Some past Events held in the Campus are RAPPELING,ZIPLINE.Moreover,once or twice in the session,Adventure Weekend is held which involves really fun and interesting Events like LASER TAG,MELT-DOWN,ARCHERY,PAINTBALL,ZORBING etc.With the tremendous reviews from the participants,TAdS-IIT KGP has always made their On-campus events successful and satisfactory for the participants.

Online Events

Technology Adventure Society,IIT KHARAGPUR,in the session 2020-21 is intiating a new platform to involve the participants in the online gaming and cheerful events.During the lockdown,TAdS-IIT KGP,had organised an online PUB-G tournament,which went successful and with satisfactory review from the participants.The society is giving a new and interesting platform to the students to make their online semester as cheerful as we can.
